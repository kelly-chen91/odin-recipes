Steamed Fish

What a fishy recipe!

This fish recipe involves with steaming the fish with ginger, scallions, and seasoned soy sauce for seafood. It only takes an half an hour to make, an easy comfort food for a quick weekday dinner or for family gathering!

This recipe works for a lot of different types of fishes. I would recommend making it with sea bass!

Ingredients

  • Fish, butchered and descaled
  • 4 slices of ginger in thin sticks
  • The white part of scallions, minced vertically
  • 2 tbsp of seasoned soy sauce for seafood

Recipe

  1. Rinse the fish under cold water and remove all the red stuff.
  2. On a plate, place the fish and stuff the fish with the slices of ginger and scallions.
  3. Boil some water with the steamer (the water should not be over the steamer) in the wok under high heat.
  4. After the water is boiling, place the plate of the fish on the steamer. Cover with the lid and steam for 15 minutes.
  5. After steaming, take out the fish and drain the fish juices. Add in the seasoned soy sauce. Enjoy!
