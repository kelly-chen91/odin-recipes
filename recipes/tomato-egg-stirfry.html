Tomato & Egg Stirfry

A delightful and traditional comfort food served in many Chinese dinner tables

This recipe is incredibly easy to make. The tangy tomatoes stir-fried with savory scrambled eggs, a hint of sugar, flavorful garlic and ginger, and fish sauce all come together to create a incredible delicious dish pair with white rice.

This is one of the first dishes that I learned how to make!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Sugar
  • Cloves of garlic, smashed
  • Ginger
  • Fish Sauce

Steps

  1. Wash the tomatoes.
  2. Slice the tomatoes to little pieces and set them aside.
  3. Crack the eggs in a bowl and scramble them with a utensil.
  4. Turn on high heat for the wok. Meanwhile, smash a few pieces of garlic and slice a few pieces of ginger
  5. When the wok is hot, add the oil and sweat the garlic and ginger for 30 seconds
  6. Add in the tomatoes and cook until tomato is semi-soft.
  7. Pour out the tomatoes in a bowl to set aside. Add oil to wok.
  8. Pour the egg into the wok. Scramble the eggs and add in the cooked tomatoes. Add in sugar and fish sauce and mix.
  9. Turn off heat and plate the dish. Enjoy!
